# Game Design Document (GDD)

Project Codename: Forgotten Wilds
Vision Statement
Create a browser-based online JRPG inspired by the wonder of classic Final Fantasy adventures, built around the myths, folklore, and landscapes of an alternate North American continent. Players explore a living world, restore harmony between humanity and ancient spirits, uncover forgotten legends, and shape the future of the world together.

## 1. Design Pillars
Every feature should support at least one of these pillars.
Explore Wonder
Players should constantly discover new places, myths, hidden paths, secrets, and stories.
Meaningful Progression
Every session should make the character stronger, wiser, or more connected to the world.
Living World
The world changes over time through seasons, community events, and story progression.
Cooperative Adventure
Multiplayer should enhance exploration and storytelling instead of replacing them.
Respect the Legend
Monsters are not simply enemies—they are part of the world’s history and culture.

## 2. Target Experience
A player logs in after work.
They meet friends in town.
Accept a new regional quest.
Travel into the Iron Mountains.
Fight ghost miners and spirit wolves.
Discover an abandoned shrine.
Help calm an angry mountain guardian.
Earn a rare Spirit Blessing.
Return to town.
Upgrade equipment.
Document new discoveries in their Journal of Legends.
Log off feeling like they experienced a memorable adventure rather than simply completed a checklist.

## 3. The World
The Continent
Working name: Asterra
An alternate continent inspired by North America.
Long ago the Great Spirits governed every forest, river, mountain, prairie, and coastline.
Human civilizations flourished alongside them.
Then came an event remembered only as The Great Silence.
The spirits withdrew.
Without their guidance:
myths became monsters
sacred places were forgotten
balance was lost
Players begin generations later as new members of an order dedicated to restoring harmony.

## 4. The Lantern Keepers
The player belongs to the Lantern Keepers.
They are explorers, historians, mediators, and protectors.
Their sacred lanterns reveal hidden paths, calm restless spirits, and preserve forgotten stories.
The Lantern Keepers do not conquer.
They understand.

## 16. Long-Term Vision
Release new regions every few months.
Expand the Journal of Legends.
Introduce new Guardian Spirits.
Add cooperative expeditions.
Grow Lodges into meaningful communities.
Allow the world to evolve through player actions while preserving handcrafted stories.
The goal is not simply to create an online RPG—it is to create a world that players feel privileged to explore, where every legend has a place, every region tells its own story, and the community helps shape the future of the Forgotten Wilds.
