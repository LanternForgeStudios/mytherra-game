# The World Bible

Forgotten Wilds
Volume I – The Creation of Mytherra
The First Promise
Before mountains stood.
Before rivers flowed.
Before people walked beneath the stars.
There was only the Endless Sky and the Deep Earth.
When Sky and Earth finally touched, sparks of living light scattered across the newborn land.
Those sparks became the First Spirits.
Some became rivers.
Some became forests.
Some became mountains.
Some became storms.
Every place possessed a living soul.
This sacred balance became known as The First Promise:
“As long as the people remember the land, the land will remember the people.”
The Age of Harmony
Thousands of years passed.
The Spirits did not rule over humanity.
Instead they taught.
People learned:
healing from the rivers
hunting from the wolves
patience from the mountains
courage from the thunder
wisdom from the old forests
The greatest among these became known as the Guardian Spirits.
Each Guardian watched over an entire region of Mytherra.
They rarely appeared openly.
Instead they whispered through dreams, weather, and wandering animals.
The Great Lantern
One Guardian saw a troubling future.
Its name has been forgotten.
It forged a lantern unlike any other.
Its light could reveal truth hidden beneath fear.
This became the First Lantern.
Those entrusted with carrying it became the first Lantern Keepers.
Their duty was simple:
Protect stories.
Protect balance.
Never seek power over the Spirits.
Only partnership.
The Great Silence
Then…
Everything changed.
No one knows why.
One autumn morning…
The Guardian Spirits vanished.
Not dead.
Not destroyed.
Silent.
Without their guidance:
Forests grew restless.
Storms became violent.
Animals transformed into monsters.
Legends twisted into nightmares.
The First Promise was broken.
This event became known simply as:
The Great Silence.
No written record explains what truly happened.
Recovering that truth becomes one of the central mysteries of the game.
The Age of Fear
Without the Guardians…
People stopped trusting the wilderness.
Cities built walls.
Roads became dangerous.
Stories became warnings instead of lessons.
Children grew up believing monsters had always existed.
Few remembered that they had once been protectors.
The Lantern Keepers
Most orders disappeared during the Great Silence.
The Lantern Keepers survived.
Their libraries became sanctuaries.
Their lanterns became symbols of hope.
Every generation they trained new Keepers.
Not as warriors.
But as explorers.
Historians.
Mediators.
Protectors of forgotten truths.
The player begins as the newest initiate.
The Five Great Principles
Every Lantern Keeper swears these vows:
Seek before judging.
Listen before fighting.
Protect every story.
Respect every spirit.
Carry the light into forgotten places.
These principles shape both the narrative and optional gameplay choices.
Guardian Spirits
The Guardians are not gods.
They are living embodiments of places.
When a Guardian suffers…
Its entire region suffers.
When one heals…
The land heals with it.
Examples include:
The Great Thunderbird
The White Buffalo
Mother Cypress
Old Stone Bear
The River Singer
Cedar Giant
Winter Stag
Many legendary creatures players first encounter as enemies may later become allies once their stories are understood.
The Echoes
When fear replaces understanding…
Spirits become corrupted.
These corrupted manifestations are called Echoes.
Echoes are the primary source of many hostile encounters.
They are not inherently evil.
They are wounded memories of what once was.
One of the recurring themes of the game is deciding when to defeat an Echo and when to restore it.
Spirit Shrines
Across Mytherra lie forgotten shrines.
Each shrine is connected to a specific Guardian or local legend.
Restoring shrines:
strengthens nearby towns
unlocks new quests
calms dangerous areas
improves regional prosperity
reveals hidden lore
changes the appearance of the environment
Players see the world gradually heal because of their actions.
Regions of Mytherra
Every region reflects a distinct aspect of American folklore and landscape.
The Iron Mountains embody endurance and memory.
The Crimson Bayou reflects mystery and transformation.
The Endless Prairie symbolizes freedom and connection.
The Whispering Pines guard ancient wisdom.
The Shattered Desert preserves forgotten civilizations.
The Frozen Frontier teaches resilience and sacrifice.
Each expansion adds another chapter to the continent’s living history.
The Journal of Legends
The Journal is considered sacred.
Lantern Keepers believe:
“A forgotten story is the first step toward losing a place forever.”
Every new discovery becomes another page in the player’s personal chronicle.
Players are not simply completing achievements.
They are preserving the memory of Mytherra itself.
The Future
Few people now believe the Guardian Spirits still exist.
Most think the Lantern Keepers are chasing fairy tales.
But strange signs are appearing once again.
Ancient lanterns are beginning to glow.
Animals behave in impossible ways.
Old shrines awaken.
The stars themselves seem to be moving.
Something is calling the Guardians back.
Whether that return brings hope or catastrophe depends on the choices made by every Lantern Keeper—including the player.
And somewhere, hidden beyond the oldest forests and highest mountains, lies the truth behind the Great Silence.
The journey to uncover that truth is only beginning.
