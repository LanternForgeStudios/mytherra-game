# Master Art Direction Prompt v1.0

Purpose
This prompt establishes the permanent visual identity of Mytherra. Every concept illustration, character portrait, environment, item icon, UI element, loading screen, and marketing image should be generated from this artistic foundation unless a specific asset specification overrides it.
Core Identity
Mytherra is a world inspired by the landscapes, folklore, and history of an alternate North America.
The world should feel ancient, hopeful, mysterious, and handcrafted.
The emotional tone is:
Wonder before danger.
Hope before darkness.
Curiosity before conflict.
Players should feel invited to explore.
Artistic Style
Painterly stylized fantasy.
Hand-painted digital illustration.
Rich natural lighting.
Soft brushwork.
Subtle texture.
Readable silhouettes.
Natural proportions.
Storybook quality.
Avoid photorealism.
Avoid anime styling.
Avoid exaggerated cartoon proportions.
Avoid excessive visual effects.
Every image should feel timeless.
Lighting
Lighting is one of the defining characteristics of Mytherra.
Use:
• Warm lantern light
• Golden sunrise
• Misty mornings
• Blue twilight
• Moonlit forests
• Soft volumetric light through trees
Lanterns should always produce a warm amber glow that symbolizes hope and safety.
Darkness should feel mysterious rather than horrific.
Color Language
Natural earth tones dominate.
Primary colors:
Forest greens
Weathered browns
Copper
Stone gray
Warm amber
Autumn orange
Moss green
Deep river blue
Muted crimson
Never oversaturate.
Reserve bright colors for sacred moments, Guardian Spirits, and Resonance effects.
Environment Design
Every environment should include four layers:
Natural landscape
Human history
Spiritual presence
Living environmental details
Examples include:
Misty pine forests
Old stone bridges
Weathered cabins
Ancient shrines
Lantern-lit paths
Flowing streams
Wildflowers
Fireflies
Smoke from chimneys
Wind-blown leaves
The world should always feel inhabited.
Character Design
Characters should appear practical and well-traveled.
Clothing reflects:
Region
Occupation
Culture
Experience
Equipment should look handcrafted and functional.
The Lantern is the signature visual element carried by every Lantern Keeper.
No oversized fantasy weapons.
No excessive armor.
No glowing magical clothing unless story appropriate.
Creature Design
Creatures are born from myths, memories, and Resonance.
Avoid generic fantasy monsters.
Each creature should communicate:
Its region.
Its emotional state.
Its history.
Its relationship with the First Ones.
Echoes should feel sorrowful rather than evil.
Guardians should inspire awe rather than fear.
Architecture
Communities should appear built over generations.
Materials:
Timber
Stone
Iron
Brick
Copper
Buildings should show repair, history, and use.
Avoid perfectly clean fantasy cities.
User Interface Inspiration
Leather journals
Handwritten notes
Old maps
Brass instruments
Wooden carvings
Wax seals
Compass roses
Pressed flowers
The interface should feel like equipment carried by a Lantern Keeper.
Mood
Quiet.
Peaceful.
Thoughtful.
Hopeful.
Melancholy without despair.
Players should constantly feel there is another story waiting just beyond the next hill.
Signature Motifs
Every major piece of artwork should contain one or more recurring Mytherra motifs whenever appropriate:
Warm lantern glow
Mountain mist
Ancient carved stones
Handwritten journals
Fireflies
Flowing water
Autumn leaves
Old pine forests
Weathered wood
Wildflowers
Copper lanterns
Sacred shrines
These motifs create instant visual recognition.
Composition
Guide the viewer’s eye naturally.
Foreground should include environmental storytelling.
Midground should establish the subject.
Background should hint at larger mysteries.
Every image should encourage the viewer to wonder what lies just outside the frame.
Negative Prompt Guidance
Avoid:
Modern objects
Science fiction technology
Cyberpunk aesthetics
Neon lighting
Floating UI elements
Overly dark horror imagery
Graphic violence
Oversized weapons
Hyper-muscular anatomy
Anime facial proportions
Plastic-looking materials
Visual clutter
Final Quality Check
Before approving any artwork, ask:
• Does this immediately feel like Mytherra?
• Is there a sense of hope?
• Is there a sense of mystery?
• Does the lighting tell a story?
• Does the environment feel alive?
• Would this image still be recognizable without any text?
If the answer is “yes” to all of the above, the artwork aligns with the visual identity of Mytherra.
